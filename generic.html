This is Me

Here you can find out some information about me and what makes me tick.

Hi, I'm Gregory Panagary — a final-year Computer Engineering student originally from the beautiful islands of Seychelles, currently based in Kuala Lumpur. I’m passionate about building smart, practical solutions through full-stack development and AI-driven systems. My work sits at the intersection of software, hardware, and intelligence, with a strong focus on machine learning, computer vision, and data analytics.

Throughout my academic journey, I’ve developed a variety of projects — from real-time AI surveillance systems and smart agriculture platforms to business analytics dashboards and defect detection using computer vision. These hands-on experiences have helped me grow as a problem solver who’s comfortable working across the tech stack, whether it’s writing backend logic, training deep learning models, or designing intuitive user interfaces.

I’m deeply motivated by the idea of using technology to solve real-world problems, especially in ways that are ethical, inclusive, and sustainable. I enjoy working on systems that automate tasks, enhance decision-making, and improve user experience — and I’m always curious to explore new tools and methods that push these possibilities further.

Beyond tech, I’m an active and creative person. I enjoy digital drawing and video editing, gaming with friends as well as listening to and discovering new music. I also like spending time outdoors — whether it’s hiking, enjoying nature, or just relaxing by the beach back home. Sports are also a big part of my life — I play football and table tennis as a way to stay disciplined, focused, and energized. Growing up in Seychelles gave me a strong appreciation for nature and balance, which continues to influence how I approach both work and life. My multicultural background and exposure to diverse environments have shaped me into someone who values empathy, adaptability, and collaboration in everything I do.
